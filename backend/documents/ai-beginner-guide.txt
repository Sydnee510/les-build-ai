========================================
GETTING STARTED WITH AI — A Beginner's Guide
========================================
For people who are brand new to AI and have no coding background.

WHAT IS AI?
-----------
AI (Artificial Intelligence) is software that can understand language, answer questions, generate text,
and complete tasks that normally require human thinking. Think of it like a really smart assistant that
you can talk to by typing.

You do NOT need to be a programmer to use AI or even build with it. Many tools today let you build
AI-powered apps with minimal or no coding experience.


WHAT IS A CHATBOT?
------------------
A chatbot is an AI you can have a conversation with. You type a message, the AI reads it, thinks about
it, and types back a response. Examples you may have used: ChatGPT, Claude, Siri, or Alexa.

The chatbot you're building today works the same way — you type a question on your website, it gets
sent to an AI model (OpenAI's GPT), and the AI's response appears on screen.


WHAT IS AN API?
---------------
An API (Application Programming Interface) is how two pieces of software talk to each other. In our
case, your app sends the user's message to OpenAI's API, and OpenAI sends back the AI's response.

Think of it like ordering at a restaurant:
  - You (the app) tell the waiter (the API) what you want
  - The waiter takes your order to the kitchen (OpenAI's servers)
  - The kitchen prepares your food (generates a response)
  - The waiter brings it back to you (returns the response)

You need an API key to use OpenAI's API — it's like your membership card that lets you place orders.


WHAT IS A PROMPT?
-----------------
A prompt is the instruction you give to the AI. There are two types in our app:

1. System Prompt — Behind-the-scenes instructions that tell the AI HOW to behave. The user never
   sees this. Example: "You are a friendly fitness coach who motivates people."

2. User Prompt — The actual message the user types in the chat box.

The better your system prompt, the better your AI will perform. Be specific! Tell it:
  - Who it is (name, role)
  - How to talk (casual, professional, funny)
  - What topics to focus on
  - What to avoid


WHAT IS A MODEL?
----------------
An AI model is the brain behind the chatbot. Different models have different capabilities:

  - gpt-4.1-mini  → Fast and affordable. Great for simple chatbots and quick answers.
  - gpt-5.4-mini  → Smarter and newer. Better at understanding complex questions.
  - gpt-5.4       → The most powerful. Best for detailed, nuanced conversations.

You can switch models in the chat.service.js file. Start with gpt-4.1-mini and upgrade later!


WHAT IS RAG?
------------
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) is a way to give your AI specific knowledge. Without RAG, the
AI only knows what it was trained on (general knowledge up to its training date).

With RAG, you can give the AI documents — like an FAQ, a menu, a product catalog, or company info —
and it will use that information to answer questions accurately.

In this app, we store documents in the /backend/documents/ folder. The AI reads them and uses them
as context when answering questions.


FIRST STEPS TO START BUILDING
------------------------------
1. Don't be afraid to experiment — you can't break anything permanently
2. Start by changing the system prompt to give your AI a personality
3. Try asking your chatbot different questions and see how it responds
4. Edit the documents in the /backend/documents/ folder to teach your AI new things
5. Change the colors and text on your website to make it yours
6. Google anything you don't understand — every developer does this constantly!

Remember: Every expert was once a beginner. You're already building with AI — that's amazing!

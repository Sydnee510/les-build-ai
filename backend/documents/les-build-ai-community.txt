========================================
LET'S BUILD AI — Community & Company Info
========================================

ABOUT US
--------
Let's Build AI is a community and workshop series dedicated to empowering women
and underrepresented groups to build with artificial intelligence. We believe that
the people shaping AI should reflect the diversity of the world it serves.


OUR MISSION
-----------
To make AI accessible to everyone — regardless of technical background. We provide
hands-on workshops, mentorship, and a supportive community where beginners can go
from zero to building real AI-powered applications in a single session.


WHAT WE OFFER
-------------
• Live Workshops — Hands-on, guided sessions where you build a real AI app from scratch. No prior experience needed.
• Community Support — A welcoming Discord community where you can ask questions, share progress, and connect with other builders.
• Resource Library — Tutorials, templates, and guides to keep learning after the workshop ends.
• Mentorship — Connect with experienced builders who can help you take your AI project to the next level.
• Startup Support — For those ready to build a real product, we offer guidance on going from prototype to launch.


OUR VALUES
----------
• Inclusion — AI should be built by everyone, for everyone.
• Accessibility — No gatekeeping. If you can type, you can build.
• Community — We grow faster together than alone.
• Action — We don't just talk about AI. We build with it.
• Empowerment — Leave every workshop more capable than when you arrived.


THE AMIELLEVIA APP
------------------
Amiellevia is an AI-powered application created by the founder of Let's Build AI.
It showcases what's possible when you combine creativity with AI technology. The app
demonstrates how the same skills taught in our workshops can be used to build real,
production-grade products that serve real users.

Amiellevia is proof that you don't need a massive team or years of experience to
build something meaningful with AI. It started as a workshop project and grew into
a full application — and your project can follow the same path.
